Privacy Policy

Snapceipt: Receipt Scanner · Last updated: July 10, 2026

Snapceipt ("we", "our", or "the app") helps you scan receipts and track your spending. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Account information. Your email address, and — if you sign in with Apple or Google — the name and email those services share with us.
  • Receipt data. Photos of receipts you scan and the information extracted from them (store names, items, prices, dates).
  • Usage data. Your monthly scan count (used to enforce free-tier limits) and in-app points.
  • Subscription status. Whether you have an active Snapceipt Pro subscription. Payments are processed entirely by Apple; we never see your payment details.

How we use your information

  • To provide the app's core features: scanning receipts, extracting their contents, storing them in your vault, and generating spending insights.
  • To manage your account and subscription.
  • To send account-related emails (such as sign-up confirmation codes and password resets).

We do not sell your data, show ads, or use your data for advertising.

Service providers

We use a small number of providers to operate the app, each receiving only what is needed for their function:

  • Supabase — authentication, database, and image storage.
  • Anthropic — receipt images are processed by the Claude AI model to extract their contents. Images are processed to provide the service and are not used to train AI models.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management.
  • Apple — payment processing for subscriptions.
  • Resend — delivery of account emails.

Data retention & deletion

Your data is kept while your account is active. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data (receipts, images, and profile) at any time in the app under Settings → Delete account. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.

Security

Your data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS) and stored with access controls so that only your account can read your receipts.

Children

Snapceipt is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes, we will update this page and the date above.
